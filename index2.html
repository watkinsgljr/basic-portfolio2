Steps to make sandwhich

  1. Get bread
  2. Get peanut butter and jelly
  3. Lather one slice of bread with peanut butter
  4. Lather the other slice with jelly
  5. Place to slices of bread down with peanut butter and jelly sides down
  6. Eat up and enjoy!!
